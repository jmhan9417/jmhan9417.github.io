Effect of a refund

A full refund revokes only the entitlement created by that purchase. A separate valid purchase or administrator grant remains active. A partial refund, if agreed in writing, retains access. Refund processing time depends on Stripe and the issuing bank and commonly takes several business days.

Disputes and duplicate charges

Contact support first if you believe a charge is duplicated or unauthorized. A payment dispute suspends the affected grant while Stripe and the card network review it. If funds are reinstated after a resolved dispute, the valid grant may be restored.

Account deletion

Deleting learning data or the account does not refund an existing fulfilled purchase merely because the account is deleted. Submit a refund request before deletion if you want that purchase reviewed under this policy. A captured pending, late, or asynchronous payment that cannot be fulfilled because account closure already started is canceled when possible or sent through the automatic refund process.

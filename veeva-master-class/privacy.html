Privacy Policy

Short version. ReadyToConsult uses your email to provide an account, stores progress when you sign in, uses Stripe for purchases, and sends only the current interview context to an AI provider when you request a Partner Review. We do not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising.

Who is responsible

ReadyToConsult is operated by Minseok Han, an individual based in California, United States. Privacy and data requests may be sent to jmhan9417@gmail.com.

Information we process

  • Account: email address, Supabase user ID, authentication timestamps, and account status.
  • Learning progress: course answers, notebook entries, interview drafts, saved evaluations, and lab state when cloud sync is enabled by signing in. Unsigned progress stays in a browser-specific local namespace.
  • Partner Review: current case and stage, current answer, immediately preceding interviewer follow-up and response, exact evidence excerpts, generated rewrite, structured scores, salted input hash, model route, token counts, and latency. The saved review can reproduce part or all of your wording.
  • Purchases: Stripe Customer, Checkout Session, PaymentIntent, price, amount, currency, refund, and dispute identifiers. ReadyToConsult does not receive or store full card details.
  • Feedback: rating, optional message, and whether you permit contact. Feedback remains private unless you later provide separate publication consent.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser details, security events, and request logs collected by hosting, authentication, AI, and payment processors.

Why we use it

We use data to authenticate users, synchronize progress, provide and secure paid access, generate requested coaching, prevent fraud and abuse, reconcile payments and refunds, answer support requests, and improve product quality from real private feedback. Depending on jurisdiction, these purposes rely on performance of a contract, consent for an optional AI request, legal obligations, and legitimate interests in security and service improvement.

Processors and external services

  • Supabase: authentication and database hosting.
  • Vercel: site hosting, serverless functions, request logs, and AI Gateway routing.
  • Configured AI model provider: processes the limited case packet and answer needed for a requested review. The provider/model route is recorded with each review.
  • Stripe: Checkout, payment, refund, dispute, receipt, and fraud processing.
  • YouTube and browser speech services: optional embedded video and speech-recognition features may transmit data under their own policies. ReadyToConsult stores text transcripts, not audio.

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where these providers operate.

Retention

  • Cloud progress, saved evaluations, and private feedback: until you delete learning data or your account.
  • Evaluator operational metadata and failed-request records: up to 30 days unless needed for abuse investigation.
  • Webhook and security logs: normally up to 90 days, longer when needed for a dispute or security incident.
  • Purchase, refund, and accounting records: up to seven years or as otherwise required by law. These can remain after account deletion in de-identified or transaction-linked form.
  • Account-closure safety record: a pseudonymous account ID plus associated Stripe customer and Checkout Session IDs for up to one year after the latest closure-related payment activity, so any late or asynchronous payment can be recognized and an automatic refund can be attempted. Failed refunds remain flagged for support follow-up. It does not retain your email or practice answers.
  • Backups: provider-managed backups follow the retention period configured for the applicable hosting plan. If a backup is restored, recorded deletion requests are reapplied where technically feasible.

Your controls and rights

Account includes a Delete learning data control for progress, evaluations, and private feedback, and a Delete account control for the Supabase identity and active access. Account deletion is permanent and removes paid access. It does not refund an existing fulfilled purchase merely because the account is deleted. A captured pending, late, or asynchronous payment that cannot be fulfilled because account closure already started is canceled when possible or sent through the automatic refund process. Financial records retained for legal purposes are not erased from Stripe or accounting records.

You may request access, correction, export, deletion, restriction, or an explanation of processing by emailing the contact above. California residents may also request to know, correct, or delete covered personal information and may exercise rights without discriminatory treatment. ReadyToConsult does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Security and limits

We use account-scoped database policies, server-side entitlement checks, signed payment webhooks, strict evaluator schemas, minimal AI context, and no-store API responses. No system is perfectly secure. Automated AI feedback can be wrong and should not include confidential employer, client, patient, or regulated information.

Children and changes

ReadyToConsult is not directed to children under 13. Material policy changes will receive a new version and effective date. Purchasers are shown the current version before Checkout.

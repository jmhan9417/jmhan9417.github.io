GenericCheck

Manufacturer-level generic drug transparency

A patient-facing registry that answers a question routine substitution hides: which manufacturer actually made your generic, and how do patient experiences differ between them?

  • 175 solid-oral drugs — including all 9 narrow-therapeutic-index drugs — with manufacturer-specific patient experiences and FDA recall data.
  • Data model grounded in MedDRA Preferred Terms and FDA Orange Book therapeutic-equivalence codes.
  • Privacy, liability, and stakeholder data-access questions resolved at the design stage, not after.

Regulatory · Promotional review · live beta

FactCheckMate

Dual-market (US + Korea) ad-claim compliance checker

Checks dietary-supplement and cosmetic advertising claims against both U.S. (FDA/FTC) and South Korean advertising standards — phrase by phrase. Built after finding no accessible consumer tool existed.

  • Three-stage pipeline: rule scan, AI context analysis, citation verification against FDA/FTC and Korean legal sources.
  • Converts regulatory review into a 0–100 auditable risk score; encodes the structure/function-vs-drug-claim line (21 CFR 101.93).
  • Designed for two user groups (consumers, sellers) with legal-risk constraints built in: private outputs, consent gate.
